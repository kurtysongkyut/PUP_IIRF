Welcome to the PUP Individual Inventory Record Form (IIRF) Help Page

For Students

  • Filling Out the Form:
    • Go to the Home page.
    • Carefully fill in all required fields (marked with * or required).
    • Double-check your information before submitting.
    • Click the Submit button at the bottom of the form.
    • You will see a confirmation message if your form is submitted successfully.
  • Updating Your Information:
    • Go to the Update page.
    • Enter your record ID or select your record (as instructed).
    • Update the necessary fields and click Update.

For Admins

  • Accessing the Dashboard:
    • Go to the Admin page and log in with your admin credentials.
  • Managing Submissions:
    • View all student submissions in a table.
    • Use the View button to see full details of a submission.
    • Click Edit to update a student's record.
    • Click Delete to remove a submission (this action is permanent).
    • Click Download to export a submission as a CSV file.
    • Use the search bar to quickly find records by name or email.
  • Logging Out:
    • Click the Logout button at the top right of the Admin Dashboard.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: I made a mistake in my submission. How do I correct it?
    A: Go to the Update page, enter your record ID, and update your information.
  • Q: I can't find my record ID.
    A: Please contact your administrator or guidance office for assistance.
  • Q: Who can see my information?
    A: Only authorized admins and guidance counselors can access your data.
